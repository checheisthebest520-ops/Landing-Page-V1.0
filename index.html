painpoints

出海痛点Pain Points

01

人才成长无体系No talent system

只能靠老板亲自带，新人培养慢、留不住，缺少可以持续输出高质量外贸人才的人才孵化机制。 Reliance on founders to train; slow onboarding and high turnover. No scalable system to develop trade talent.

02

有资源 / 难整合Resources, hard to align

买家、工厂、团队资源分散在各个渠道，缺乏一个能同时看懂需求、供应链与人才的“总控平台”。 Buyers, factories, and teams are siloed. Missing one platform that sees demand, supply chain, and talent together.

03

项目多 / 难复制Projects, hard to scale

订单更多依赖一次性机会，缺少从项目到方法论的沉淀，无法形成可复用的“铁三角”服务模型。 Deals depend on one-off opportunities. Little reuse of playbooks or a repeatable “talent–supply–buyer” model.
